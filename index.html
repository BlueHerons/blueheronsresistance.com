Welcome, Agent.

Congratulations on joining the Right side – The Resistance. Your dedication to the future of Humanity will not go unnoticed.

We are the Blue Herons. We are fighting to resist the Shaper’s ingress in Puget Sound. If you have not done so yet, you can join us by downloading the scanner app for Android or iOS and joining the Resistance faction when you start.

The Seattle Resistance Google+ community is where we organize meetups, swap gear, and coordinate team efforts. There are enemy forces everywhere. You must be vetted. Should traitors be discovered, they will be dealt with swiftly and justly.

Before you can join, we need to verify your Resistance status please follow these steps.

1) Request to join on the Seattle Resistance community page.

2) Go to the Faction COMM on the Ingress Intel Map or using your scanner (the Ingress App) on your phone or tablet.

3) Type “@woodlandmonk - [Your Google+ FirstName LastName] for Seattle Resistance”.

4) One of our moderators will approve your join request as soon as we are able. If your request still has not been approved after 24 hours, please resend the above message or reach out to other Resistance Agents in the COMM.

5) Once you have bee approved, please introduce yourself to the community in the Agent Profiles section. We have mentor agents that can help you get started but we can’t help if we don’t know who or where you are!

See you in the field, Agent,
